
Privacy Policy for XP Tracker

Effective Date: April 15, 2025

XP Tracker is a fitness progress tracker app developed to help users build healthy workout habits. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data.

1. Information We Collect
We collect the following information:
- Account credentials (email + password for login, stored securely)
- Workout logs, XP, level, and streak progress
- No personal health data, location, or contact info is collected

2. How We Use Information
We use your data solely to:
- Track your workout progress and award XP
- Maintain login functionality
- Display your current level and streak

3. Data Sharing
We do not share your personal data with any third parties.

4. Data Storage
Data is stored securely and only used within the app. XP Tracker does not collect or transmit sensitive data to any external server.

5. Your Rights
You can delete your app data at any time by removing the app. Future versions may include manual reset/delete functions.

6. Contact

7. Changes
This policy may be updated as the app evolves. Changes will be reflected here.

XP Tracker respects your privacy and values your trust.
